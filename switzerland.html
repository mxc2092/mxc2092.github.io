Introduction

Tour Switzerland in 60 seconds!

The Swiss Confederation was founded as a defensive alliance among three cantons.

The Swiss Confederation secured its independence from the Holy Roman Empire. A constitution with a centralized federal government replaced the confederation.

Switzerland was not involved in either of the two world wars. Switzerland's ties with its neighbors has been strengthened because of the prominent role in many UN and international organizations. Switzerland still participates in many UN and international organizations but still maintains a strong commitment to neutrality.
